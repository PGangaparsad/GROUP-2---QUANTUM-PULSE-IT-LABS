Who are we? Empowering The Nation



Precious Radebe, the person who is responsible for Empowering The Nation wants to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. Her parents and other elderly relatives were never given the chance to upskill themselves or follow a formal educational qualification so this training school is her way of supporting similarly affected members from her community



Empowering The Nation is an SME that offers programs to customers who can select from a wide variety of courses available from which customers can develop or improve their skills which can help them with setting up their own business or making them more desirable when seeking employment. Empowering the nation is offering two sets of packages of courses. The first package of courses can take over six weeks to complete and the second package of courses can take over six months to complete.

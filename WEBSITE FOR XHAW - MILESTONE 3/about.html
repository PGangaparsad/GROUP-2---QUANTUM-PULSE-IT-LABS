Who are we? Empowering The Nation Story

Empowering The Nation is a vocational training institution founded by Precious Radebe. Our mission is to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners, with a focus on empowering those who have been historically underserved in terms of educational opportunities.

Precious Radebe, inspired by her family's experience, established this training school to support individuals in her community who have not had the chance to upskill themselves or pursue formal education.

We offer a diverse range of courses designed to help individuals develop and enhance their skills, making them more competitive in the job market or better prepared to start their own businesses.

At Empowering The Nation, we offer two sets of course packages. The first package can be completed in six weeks, while the second package spans six months, allowing for more in-depth learning and skill development.
